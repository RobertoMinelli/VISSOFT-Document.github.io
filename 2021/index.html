COVID-19 Announcement

The IEEE VISSOFT 2021 Organizing Committee and IEEE Computer Society have been monitoring the developing COVID-19 situation. The safety and well-being of all conference participants is our top priority. After studying and evaluating the announcements, guidance, and news released by relevant national departments, we are announcing that the IEEE VISSOFT 2021, originally planned to take place physically in Luxembourg City will now be converted into an all-digital conference experience. More details about the organization will be announced soon.

About IEEE VISSOFT 2021

The ninth IEEE Working Conference on Software Visualization (VISSOFT 2021) builds upon the success of the previous eight editions of VISSOFT, which in turn followed after six editions of the IEEE International Workshop on Visualizing Software for Understanding and Analysis (VISSOFT) and five editions of the ACM Symposium on Software Visualization (SOFTVIS). In 2021, VISSOFT will take place and be co-located with ICSME.
