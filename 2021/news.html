VISSOFT 2021 Goes Virtual
2021/01/18

VISSOFT 2021 scheduled to be held in-person in Luxembourg City will now be converted into an all-digital conference experience. More details about the organization will be announced soon. Since we share the system with ICSME, please refer to the ICSME website for any additional details and updates.

Special Journal Issue Confirmed
2021/01/17

We're glad to announce that a selection of best papers from VISSOFT'21 will be invited to submit extended versions for tentative publication in a Special Section of the journal of Information and Software Technology published by Elsevier. More details will be announced to authors after the paper reviews.

The Call for Papers for VISSOFT 2021 is out now!
2021/01/14

The deadline for submission of technical articles and NIER / TD Track will be June 18, 2021 (Abstract on June 11). The paper submission for Journal First Track will be on July 2, 2021.

Program Committee Announced
2021/01/13

Information regarding the Program Committee members has been provided on the Committee page.

Important Dates Announced
2020/12/14

Important dates for Research, NIER/Tool Demos, and Journal First Track are provided on the Submission page.

Website Is Live
2020/12/10

The official VISSOFT 2021 website has gone live. We will successively integrate additional information once available. Stay tuned!
