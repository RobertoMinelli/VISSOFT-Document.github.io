Previous Editions

The 2021 edition of the Working Conference on Software Visualization VISSOFT was preceded by eight editions of the VISSOFT working conference, six editions of the VISSOFT workshop, and five editions of SOFTVIS, as follows:

IEEE Working Conference on Software Visualization (VISSOFT)

  • VISSOFT 2020, September 28-29, Adelaide, Australia
  • VISSOFT 2019, September 30-October 1, Cleveland, USA
  • VISSOFT 2018, September 24-25, Madrid, Spain
  • VISSOFT 2017, September 18-19, Shanghai, China
  • VISSOFT 2016, October 3-4, Raleigh, North Carolina, USA
  • VISSOFT 2015, September 27-28, Bremen, Germany
  • VISSOFT 2014, September 29-30, Victoria, CA
  • VISSOFT 2013, September 27-28, Eindhoven, Netherlands

IEEE International Workshops on Visualizing Software for Understanding and Analysis (VISSOFT)

  • VISSOFT 2011, September 29-30, Williamsburg, Virginia, USA
  • VISSOFT 2009, September 25, Edmonton, Canada
  • VISSOFT 2007, June 25-26, Banff, Canada
  • VISSOFT 2005, September 25, Budapest, Hungary
  • VISSOFT 2003, September 22, Amsterdam, Netherlands
  • VISSOFT 2002, June 26, Paris, France

ACM Symposia on Software Visualization (SOFTVIS)

  • SOFTVIS 2010, October 25-25, Salt Lake City, Utah, USA
  • SOFTVIS 2008, September 16-17, Herrsching am Ammersee, Germany
  • SOFTVIS 2006, September 4-5, Brighton, United Kindom
  • SOFTVIS 2005, September 14-15, Saint Louis, Missouri, USA
  • SOFTVIS 2003, June 11-13, San Diego, California, USA
