About IEEE VISSOFT 2022

The tenth IEEE Working Conference on Software Visualization (VISSOFT 2022) builds upon the success of the previous nine editions of VISSOFT, which in turn followed after six editions of the IEEE International Workshop on Visualizing Software for Understanding and Analysis (VISSOFT) and five editions of the ACM Symposium on Software Visualization (SOFTVIS). In 2022, VISSOFT will take place and be co-located with ICSME.
